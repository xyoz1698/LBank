The criticism isn't negligible though. Several recurring issues appear across review platforms:

**Customer Support Response Times**: This is probably the most frequent complaint. Users report waiting days or even weeks for responses to complex issues, particularly around account restrictions or withdrawal problems. The support team seems stretched thin, especially for non-English speakers.

**Withdrawal Issues**: Some users describe delays in processing withdrawals, frozen accounts pending verification, or unclear communication about withdrawal restrictions. While not universal, these reports are concerning enough to note.

**KYC Policy Changes**: Several complaints involve sudden KYC requirements for accounts that previously traded without verification. Users in certain jurisdictions found themselves locked out until completing verification, which frustrated those who valued the no-KYC feature.

**Account Restrictions**: A subset of users report accounts being restricted or funds frozen, allegedly for "risk control" or "unusual trading activity." The exchange's explanations in these cases often lack detail, leaving users confused and frustrated.

**Transparency Gaps**: The absence of Proof of Reserves and limited public information about the team behind LBank concerns some users. In an industry where transparency builds trust, this opacity raises questions.
